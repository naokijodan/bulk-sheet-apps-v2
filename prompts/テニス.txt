EXCEPTION: If PSA / JSA / Beckett Authentication cert# is provided and

use CANNOT_LIST.

TRIGGER 3 — FORGED_AUTOGRAPH_TOURNAMENT
Condition: Item claims to be official merchandise of Wimbledon /

Legal basis: eBay Counterfeit policy; trademark rights of Grand Slam events.
Detection signals: item bears official tournament branding but source
indicates 非公式 / コピー / レプリカ with false official claim.
EXCEPTION: Genuine official merchandise (with official licensing markings,

WILSON PRO STAFF RF97 / WILSON PRO STAFF — HIGH COUNTERFEIT RISK:
Genuine markers: Weight 340g unstrung (plus/minus 5g tolerance); RDC
stiffness rating approx 63 RA; grip pallets engraved with Wilson W emblem;
hologram sticker on butt cap (high-definition, color-shifting); "RF"
embossed on throat of RF97 models; bumper guard solid and tightly fitted.
Fake signals: Weight significantly under 330g; grip cap hollow or
lightweight; hologram flat / printed not shifting; "WILSON" font
inconsistency on frame; frame flexes unusually; cheap butt cap plastic.
Action: If source lacks weight or seller has not verified — do NOT list
as authentic. If fake signals detected in source → CANNOT_LIST TRIGGER 1.

BABOLAT PURE DRIVE / PURE AERO — HIGH COUNTERFEIT RISK:
Genuine markers: Dual hologram stickers (shaft + butt cap); grommet
color exactly matching official color (e.g., Pure Aero = yellow grommets,
NOT orange-yellow); paint texture matte on grip section, glossy on frame;
font weight on "Babolat" logo consistent and crisp; Woofer grommet system
integrated without gaps.
Fake signals: Single hologram or no hologram; grommet color off; logo
font thin or blurry; plastic grommets misaligned; light frame weight
(under 270g for Pure Drive = fake, genuine unstrung 300g).
Action: If dual hologram not confirmed in source → note as unverified.

YONEX VCORE / EZONE — COUNTERFEIT RISK:
Genuine markers: Official Yonex hologram (side of handle); Yonex quality
seal in grip pallet; serial number on throat area; consistent Isometric
head shape geometry.
Reference: Check Yonex official counterfeit identification guide.
Action: If hologram unconfirmed → note as "hologram status unknown."

HEAD SPEED / RADICAL / PRESTIGE — COUNTERFEIT RISK:
Genuine markers: HEAD logo embossing consistent on both sides; graphene
layering visible at throat; bumper guard labeled with HEAD model number;
string holes evenly spaced with smooth finishing; weight within 10g
of published spec.
Action: Verify source mentions receipt or purchase documentation for
high-value HEAD frames (Prestige MP 630g strung area).

PRINCE — MODERATE RISK:
Prince Phantom / Textreme series: verify textreme weave visible at 3 and
9 o'clock positions of hoop. If source does not confirm → omit textreme
claims.

GENERAL FAKE SIGNALS (any brand):
- Unusually low price vs retail (brand new under 30% of MSRP)
- "China-made" without official factory designation
- Missing model sticker or replacement aftermarket decal
- Paint bleeding at logo areas
- Uneven string hole drilling

§10 DEFECT MAPPING
========================================

Map Japanese defect terms to standard English. Include ALL defects stated
in source. Omitting defects = Misrepresentation policy violation.

Frame damage:
  フレーム割れ / クラック → "frame crack" (CRITICAL — must disclose)
  フレーム欠け / チッピング → "frame chip" or "paint chip on frame"
  フレーム反り / 歪み → "frame warped" or "frame deformed"
  塗装剥げ / 傷 → "paint chip" or "surface scratches"
  バンパーガード欠損 → "bumper guard missing / damaged"

Grommet / string hole damage:
  グロメット割れ → "cracked grommets"
  グロメット欠損 → "missing grommets"
  ストリングホール摩耗 → "string holes worn"

Grip damage:
  グリップ摩耗 / すり減り → "grip worn"
  グリップ交換 → "replacement grip"
  オーバーグリップ使用 → "overgrip applied (original grip unknown)"
  グリップ剥がれ → "grip peeling"

String condition:
  ストリング切れ → "strings broken / missing"
  ガット劣化 → "strings aged / degraded"
  未張り → "unstrung"
  張上済み → "strung" (specify brand/gauge if source states)

General cosmetic:
  汚れ → "surface dirt"
  スレ傷 → "scuff marks"
  変色 → "discoloration"
  日焼け → "sun fading"

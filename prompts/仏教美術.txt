§2 SAFETY CLASSIFIER (READ FIRST — DO NOT VIOLATE)
========================================

--- CANNOT_LIST TRIGGERS ---
If ANY of the following conditions is detected, output EXACTLY:
CANNOT_LIST: [TRIGGER_NAME]
Do NOT generate Title / Description / ProductName / Category. Stop immediately.

TRIGGER 1 — NATIONAL_CULTURAL_PROPERTY
Condition: Item is designated as 国宝 (National Treasure) / 重要文化財 (Important
Cultural Property) / 重要美術品 (Important Art Object) under Japanese law.
Legal basis: 文化財保護法 第44条 — export absolutely prohibited.
Detection signals: source text contains 国宝 / 重文 / 重要文化財 / 重要美術品 /
文化財指定 / 指定文化財.
Note: Genuine Unkei / Kaikei attributions are almost certainly designated ICP/NT.
Any authentic Kamakura master attribution = treat as CANNOT_LIST risk.

TRIGGER 2 — CITES_IVORY
Condition: Primary material is elephant ivory (象牙 / 牙 / ivory).
Legal basis: CITES Appendix I; eBay global ivory ban since 2009 (no antique
exception for objects whose primary material is ivory).
Applies to: 象牙仏 / 牙仏 / 象牙数珠 / 象牙根付 / 象牙製仏具.
Exception: mammoth ivory — also avoid (eBay enforcement risk; authentication
requires expert documentation not feasible in listing process).

TRIGGER 3 — CITES_BEKKO_TORTOISESHELL
Condition: Material is 鼈甲 (bekko) / 玳瑁 (hawksbill tortoiseshell).
Legal basis: CITES Appendix I (Eretmochelys imbricata) — eBay global ban.
Applies to: 鼈甲装飾 / 鼈甲製仏具.

TRIGGER 4 — CITES_WHALEBONE
Condition: Material is 鯨骨 (whale bone) or 人骨 (human bone/relic).
Legal basis: CITES Appendix I (Physeteridae / Balaenidae) — eBay Animal Products
Policy explicitly prohibits sperm whale products.
Applies to: 鯨骨念珠 / 人骨数珠 / 仏舎利 labeled as human bone.

--- ARTIST RISK TIER ---

TIER 1 — HIGH RISK (never claim without documentation):
Ancient masters (public domain BUT genuine works = almost certainly designated
ICP/NT = CANNOT_LIST for export):
  Jocho (定朝, died 1057) — perfected yosegi-zukuri

  Kaikei (快慶, died c.1230) — genuine works all NT/ICP; export impossible
  Tankei (湛慶, 1173-1256) — Unkei's son; works designated
  Unkaku (運覚) / Kokei (康慶) — Kei-ha school; all ICP risk

Edo masters (public domain; forgeries extremely common):
  Enku (円空, 1632-1695) — natabori hatchet-carving style; vowed 120,000 statues;
    ~5,000 confirmed; forgeries are widespread.
    Authentic: bold decisive strokes, no hesitation, natural aged patina.
    Fake: hesitant strokes, coal-tar artificial aging.
    Auction range: USD 5,376-239,400 (Christie's NY 2022 record).
    Authentication: contact regional Enku research societies.
  Mokujiki (木喰, 1718-1810) — bisho-butsu smiling Buddhas; 626 extant confirmed;
    warm rounded carving style; forgeries less common than Enku.

Modern (copyright protected):
  Matsuhisa Sorin (松久宗琳, 1912-1992) — COPYRIGHT until 2062.
    Enryakuji Five Jnana Buddhas; Shitennoji statue; workshop still active.

§19 CONDITION TERMS (古美術業界 4 段階)
========================================

New: Unused condition. No signs of age or wear. Contemporary piece or
  museum-quality reproduction in unworn state.
Used: Normal condition with age-appropriate wear consistent with period.
  Lacquer surface intact; no significant structural issues.
Acceptable: Age deterioration present. Some lacquer loss, gold leaf fading,
  minor repairs, or woodworm damage that does not compromise structural
  integrity. Priced accordingly.
For parts or not working: Significant structural damage, major missing parts
  (head, limbs, halo, pedestal), or condition suitable for restoration
  or parts use only.

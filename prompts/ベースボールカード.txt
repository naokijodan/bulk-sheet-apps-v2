=== CONDITION TERMS (TCG Standard — 6 grades) ===
Use these EXACT terms for raw (ungraded) card condition. Do NOT use hobby terms (Mint/Near Mint/Excellent/Good/Poor).

New — Factory sealed; never removed from pack
NM — Near Mint: minimal or no visible wear; sharp corners; no creases
LP — Lightly Played: minor edge/corner wear; no creases; image clear
MP — Moderately Played: visible wear on edges/corners; minor scratches acceptable
HP — Heavily Played: major wear; creases or heavy scratches present; still intact
DMG — Damaged: card is bent/torn/extremely worn; significantly affects appearance or play
